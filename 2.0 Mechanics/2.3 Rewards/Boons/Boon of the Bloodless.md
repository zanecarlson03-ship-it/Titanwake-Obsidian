## Description
This[[Boons | boon]] grants a creature the ability to become unrestricted by water, allowing it to walk along the ocean floor. This boon takes a toll on the body, as it requires the user to be drained of blood and the body must be preserved with a special mystic art known only by [[The Iekita Tribe]].

## Effect
The creature can move in water as though it is air. The creature loses the ability to swim, but retains any ability that allows it to fly. If the creature does not return its blood to its body in time, the mystic art will wear off, killing the creature instantly. The creature may eat foods and drink liquids of a higher density than water.
