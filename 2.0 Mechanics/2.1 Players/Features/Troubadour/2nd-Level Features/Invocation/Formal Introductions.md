      calling card or a formal letter) addressed to an enemy. You can deliver

      measures to stop you. The Director gains 1 additional Malice per combat
      round during encounters involving the target. The heroes start each such
      encounter with 2 additional hero tokens (Chapter 1: The Basics). These
      hero tokens disappear at the end of the encounter.
